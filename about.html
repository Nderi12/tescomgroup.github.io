About Us


Ascom Networks Limited was incorporated as a Limited Liability Company in Nairobi, Kenya. The Company's primary focus is to provide engineering services in Telecommunication industry, ranging from conceptual planning, design level, implementation and technical support.
The Company has a complete design and build capabilities for retail, commercial and industrial telecommunication environments. We consistently continue to represent expertise, innovation and reliable service and prides on the reputation of excellence we have established by serving big corporate clients in Africa. The Company has successfully completed many projects in Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda, Nigeria and has a strong international presence which continues to grow each day.
Our design and installation specialists bring to your project a perfect blend of high technology innovativeness and work ethics. It has a solid experience and an enviable workforce of highly motivated engineers and technicians.

Human Resource Policy

Ascom considers its staff as the most important asset and are regarded as partners in business. It therefore devotes considerable resources towards ensuring for their well being and establishment of a productive working environment. The Company is committed to hiring staff regardless of gender, race, creed, marital status or national origin.


Ascom actively encourages opportunities for personal growth, discourages all forms of discrimination, and provides a safe well-equipped environment and an opportunity for staff to participate in matters that affect them and their work. The company endeavors to provide it's staff with relevant, and effective training in pursuit of the highest levels of competence and service quality at all times. To meet our staff members' needs, we:

* create a sense of belonging in an environment of mutual trust, respect, and dignity;
* have developed the tools, training, and education for each member, recognizing individual skills and knowledge.
* believe in creative, motivated, responsible team members who understand that change is critical to success,
* have in place various policies and procedures which guide us in the management of our human resources.
