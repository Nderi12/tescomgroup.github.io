Welcome to Tescom Group Ltd

Tescom Group is a consortium of companies offering Engineering Services and Solutions. Our primary objective is to provide high quality products & services to a wide range of clientele in:

- Civil Engineering, Building & Construction

- Telecommunication Infrastructure

- Electricity Infrastructure

Tescom Group and its subsidiaries has been at the forefront in engineering projects in East Africa for over 5 years. We continue and remain active in infrastructural development in the region.

Tescom Group has successfully undertaken projects in Civil Works constructions, Radio Frequency (RF) & Microwave Engineering, Construction Projects, Energy Transmission and Distribution, Operations & Maintenance for both telecoms and energy industry.

In addition, Tescom provides highly trained, competent, and motivated human resources to undertake consultancy and training across multiple domains.

Tescom Group has a market vision of becoming a leader in provision of customer focused innovative technologies in providing end to end solutions to Infrastructure development in Africa.

We have positioned ourselves to become a one-stop shop for all Infrastructure needs in the region.
