Telecommunication Engineering

Civil Works

Ascom undertakes a complete turnkey solution in telecommunication site construction. Our services include:

1.Provision of Technical Site Survey (Multi-skilled Site Visit)
2.Geotechnical tests and analysis
3.Structural site designs
4.Foundation works of rooftop and Ground base tower along with shelter and DG foundations
5.Peripheral works like boundary wall, fencing works, Guard room, toilets, approach road, retaining walls in hilly areas etc.
6.All electrical works, DG installation, Electric fence etc.

Installation and Commissioning Services

Ascom has competent installation and commissioning teams to provide quality works to the customers.

We provide installation services for the following systems:

1. All microwave radio systems:
- from indoor to outdoor works and aligning to achieve the required receive levels. We handle systems ranging from PDH to SDH systems, short haul to long haul systems, from single paths to Space Diversity links, from IF to wave guide systems.

2. Cellular systems:
-We install BSC, BTS, Node B and RNC. We handle all indoor and outdoor works and commission the systems to the designed specifications.

3. VSAT System:
-We install VSAT antennas and align it to the required lookup angle, VSAT modems and commission the link to the required design parameters.

4. NSM systems:
-We install and integrate NMS system: modeling the network elements, installing the back haul DCN network and activation of the elements on the NMS.
